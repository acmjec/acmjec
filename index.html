About Us

ACM at Jabalpur Engineering College brings together computing educators, researchers, and professionals to inspire dialogue, share resources, and address the field's challenges.

As the world’s largest computing society, ACM strengthens the profession's collective voice through strong leadership, promotion of the highest standards, and recognition of technical excellence. ACM supports the professional growth of its members by providing opportunities for life‐long learning, career development, and professional networking.

Our Goals

Let’s come together to build a society where coding is in the aura, research in our minds, technology is all hearts and problems all solved.

Providing Research Guidance and Mentorship

We aim to provide Mentorship and Guidance for research, publications and other ethical practices among students .

Fostering the Culture of Computer Science

We aim to foster the culture of coding, developing and contributing by enhancing competitiveness in the institute.

Building Networks

ACM being an International community leads to diverse network expansion of individuals, by connecting the practitioners and researchers together.

  • CodePentry
    An event organized for all hardcore and newbie coders to explore and polish their skills.It helped our coders to empower logistics, increase thier problem solving ability alongwith technical proficiency.
  • Open Source
    This session was hosted by Ms. Deepika Upadhyay, Software Engineer at RedHat, GSOC Mentor & Outreachy Intern. Our speaker touched upon topics from how to start with open source, to discussing projects one can opt for and moving to exploring all amazing initiatives like GSoc, GSoD and hacktoberfest, etc.
  • Republic Coders
    One doesn't practice his freedom only via his actions, sometimes it's just thoughts. With Republic coders, the team of JEC ACM try to provide our members the freedom of their thoughts in the field of tech. Task of making a website with a patriotic theme was given to the participants. Thus combining code with the idea of benefiting our society by our talents.
  • WCE Hackathon
    A 24- hours long Front end Web Designing Contest was held for all the tech design lovers of our college. It was all about empowering the creative, technically proficient & future business-savvy budding Web developers. The nail biting competition was judged on the basis of content, originality, technological aspects and interactivity of the submitted designs.
  • AI & Cloud Computing
    The growth in the demand of AWS knowledge by companies has increased drastically, to bridge the gaps WCE ACM team organized a 2 day workshop on cloud computing and its applications using AWS. The speaker for the session Shivshakti Singh handed a project to the attendees along with her knowledge about AWS, cloud computing and more.
  • Journey To Sillicon Valley
    The much awaited speaker session by renowned Mr. Sumit Vishwakarma was held on 10th September, 2021. Students and members of the society not only cleared all their doubts but received some very great insights about the future of tech and innovation from one of our greatest and inspiring alumni.
